Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

DGN Games respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Ripple Rush handles information.

Information Collection

Ripple Rush does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

Advertising

Ripple Rush uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect limited device-related information to provide and improve advertising services in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Game Data

Game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device. DGN Games does not collect or store this information.

Children's Privacy

Ripple Rush is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
